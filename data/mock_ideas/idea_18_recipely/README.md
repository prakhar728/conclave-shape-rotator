# Recipely

A community recipe-sharing mobile app. Users post recipes with photos,
ingredients, and step-by-step instructions; the feed surfaces recipes
based on dietary preferences (vegan, gluten-free, low-carb), available
pantry items, and time-to-cook.

The killer feature is the meal planner: pick recipes for the week, and
Recipely generates a consolidated shopping list grouped by grocery store
aisle, with quantities adjusted for serving size. Optional Instacart
integration lets you order the whole list in two taps.

Monetization: free tier with ads; $4.99/mo premium removes ads, unlocks
nutrition tracking, and adds AI meal suggestions based on what's already
in your fridge.

We've validated with 200 alpha users; weekly retention is 40% which is
strong for a content app.

Stack: React Native, Firebase backend, Algolia for recipe search, OpenAI
for meal suggestions, Instacart Developer Platform for cart handoff.
